I'm Nesli (nés-lee), nice to meet you. I was born and raised in Istanbul and later moved to the Netherlands for my studies. Not long after, I found myself settled in Amsterdam.

After years of crafting physical spaces as an architect, I’ve shifted gears into front-end development. Armed with a Masters degree in Architecture, Urbanism and Building Sciences, I’ve also worked on mastering React, JavaScript, and responsive design. I bring a unique blend of architectural precision and creativity to coding, ready to build digital experiences with the same mastery forged over years of shaping physical structures.
